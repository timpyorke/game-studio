## Project: NEON

An experimental rhythm-action game with neon aesthetics.

### About

A bold experiment combining rhythm game mechanics with fast-paced action. Every beat matters, every move counts.

### Concept

Players navigate through cyberpunk-inspired levels where timing is everything. Attack, dodge, and jump to the beat of an electrifying soundtrack.

### Current Status

**Early Prototype** - Testing core mechanics and gameplay feel

### Planned Features

- Beat-synchronized combat system
- Original electronic soundtrack
- Visual spectacle with reactive neon visuals
- Speedrun-friendly level design
- Custom song support (maybe?)

### Development Notes

This is an experimental project. We're exploring what works and what doesn't. Follow development on our devlog!
