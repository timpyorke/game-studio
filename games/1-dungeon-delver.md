## Dungeon Delver

A classic roguelike dungeon crawler with modern quality-of-life improvements.

### About

Venture deep into procedurally generated dungeons filled with monsters, traps, and treasure. Every death is permanent, but knowledge is forever.

### Key Features

- **Traditional Roguelike**: Turn-based combat with permadeath
- **100+ Monsters**: Each with unique behaviors and abilities
- **Deep Item System**: Hundreds of weapons, armor, and magical artifacts
- **Multiple Classes**: 8 playable classes with distinct playstyles
- **Daily Challenges**: Compete on leaderboards with seeded runs
