## Starlight Odyssey

A 2D Action-RPG that combines classic space exploration with modern roguelike elements.

### About

Explore a vast procedurally generated universe in this epic space adventure. Build your ship, recruit a crew, and uncover the secrets of the ancients.

### Key Features

- **Procedurally Generated Universe**: Every playthrough offers unique star systems to explore
- **Ship Customization**: Deep customization system with 100+ modules and upgrades
- **Crew Management**: Recruit and manage up to 8 crew members with unique abilities
- **Story-Driven**: Rich narrative with multiple endings based on your choices
- **Co-op Multiplayer**: Team up with friends in 2-4 player co-op mode
